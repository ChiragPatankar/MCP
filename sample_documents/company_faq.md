# ClientSphere Company FAQ

## General Information

### What is ClientSphere?
ClientSphere is an AI-powered customer support platform that helps businesses provide intelligent, automated customer service through chatbots and knowledge base integration.

### Who can use ClientSphere?
ClientSphere is designed for businesses of all sizes, from startups to enterprises, who want to improve their customer support efficiency and provide 24/7 automated assistance.

## Features & Functionality

### What file types can I upload to the knowledge base?
We support the following file formats:
- PDF documents (.pdf)
- Microsoft Word documents (.doc, .docx)
- Plain text files (.txt)
- Markdown files (.md)

Maximum file size is 50MB per document.

### How does the AI chatbot work?
Our chatbot uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology. It:
1. Searches your knowledge base for relevant information
2. Retrieves the most relevant document chunks
3. Generates answers based on your actual content
4. Provides citations so customers know the source
5. Refuses to answer questions outside your knowledge base

### What languages do you support?

## Integration & Setup

### How do I integrate ClientSphere with my website?
Integration is simple:
1. Go to Widget settings in your dashboard
2. Copy the provided JavaScript snippet
3. Paste it into your website's HTML (before the closing </body> tag)
4. The chat widget will appear automatically

## Troubleshooting

### The chatbot is not responding
If your chatbot isn't responding:
1. Check if the RAG backend is online (status indicator in dashboard)
2. Verify your knowledge base has documents uploaded
3. Check your API key is configured correctly
4. Review error logs in the dashboard

### Answers are not accurate
To improve answer accuracy:
1. Upload more relevant documents to your knowledge base
2. Ensure documents are well-structured and clear
3. Use specific, detailed questions
4. Review and update your knowledge base regularly

### I'm getting quota exceeded errors
This means you've reached your monthly chat limit:
- Check your usage in Billing & Usage dashboard
- Upgrade your plan for more chats
- Wait until next billing cycle for limit reset
